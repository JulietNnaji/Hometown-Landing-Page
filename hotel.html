1. The Yacht Hotel

Located in Lagos, 1.9 km from Nike Art Gallery, The Yacht Hotel Sea Front Lekki Phase 1 provides accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness center and a bar. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The property has a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel, all rooms come with a desk. Every room is equipped with air conditioning, a safety deposit box and a flat-screen TV, and certain units at The Yacht Hotel Sea Front Lekki Phase 1 have a balcony. At the accommodation every room comes with bed linen and towels. The Yacht Hotel Sea Front Lekki Phase 1 offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Ikoyi Golf Course is 6.6 km from the hotel, while Red Door Gallery is 8 km away. The nearest airport is Murtala Muhammed International, 32.2 km from The Yacht Hotel Sea Front Lekki Phase 1, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Couples in particular like the location - they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip.

2. Villa Monument Hotel

Set in Lagos, 2.6 km from Landmark Beach, Villa Monument Hotel offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a bar. This 5-star hotel offers room service and a shared lounge. The accommodation provides nightclub and a 24-hour front desk. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. The units in Villa Monument Hotel are equipped with bathrobes and an iPod docking station. The accommodation offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Villa Monument Hotel offers a terrace. Red Door Gallery is less than 1 km from the hotel, while Ikoyi Golf Course is 3.1 km away. The nearest airport is Murtala Muhammed International Airport, 25 km from Villa Monument Hotel.

3. Bogobiri House

Bogobiri House is much more than a boutique hotel in high brow Ikoyi, Lagos. They offer friends and visitors a unique opportunity to interface with the creative soul of Africa's most vibrant city. Their recent expansion and newly upgraded rooms are perfect for business and leisure travelers. In close to two decades of operation, they have become a hub for the promotion and appreciation of art, music and local knowledge whilst not compromising their core values: First class service, security and utmost comfort. Free wifi service available.
