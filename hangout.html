Top 12 places to hangout in Lagos

Lagos is stressfull everyday. If you don't pick out time to hang out, you would miss out on all the juicy places it has to offer. Below is a list of places you can go to catch all the vibes that Lagos has to offer. Let's go!
LEKKI CONSERVATION CENTRE

Lekki Conservation Centre in Nigeria has a 401-metre-long canopy walkway which is referred to as the longest canopy walkway in Africa. The 21-year-old walkway is a quite intriguing climb and is 100% safe too. It is also one of the most visited tourist attractions in Lagos.

NIKE ART GALLERY

Nike Art Gallery is an art gallery in Lagos and Osun state owned by Nike Davies-Okundaye. The gallery is one of the largest of its kind in West Africa, it boasts a collection of about 8,000 diverse artworks from various Nigerian artists and is the perfect spot to learn more about African art and crafts.

OMU RESORT
The world of OMU RESORT is set in a rainforest environment, you get to experience nature. A home to a zoo, amusement rides and you can even go on a quad bike for more fun. Whether you choose to go alone or with friends, you'll have the time of your life at Omu resort. Although, its quite a distance from the hustle and bustle of the city, you can book a lodge and spend a few days here.

UPBEAT RECREATION CENTRE

Upbeat Recreation Centre is West Africa's First Trampoline Park that offers wide range of fun and fitness activities such as Trampolining, Wall Climbing, Foam Pit, Slam Dunk, Basketball, Football, Volleyball, a Gym and a lovely waterside restaurant. Upbeat is the perfect place to get your adrenaline pumping with an array of fun activities.

TARKWA BAY BEACH

Tarkwa Bay is an artificial sheltered beach located near the Lagos harbour in Nigeria. Due to its island status, it is only accessible by boat or water taxis. The beachis popular with swimmers and water-sports enthusiasts.

JAZZHOLE

A bookshop and record store in the heart of Ikoyi, Jazzhole is the perfect relaxing atmosphere. Lovers of books and contemporary music will find this place amazing. It provides the right ambience for book lovers with their diverse collection of books and records. Shop for books, read, buy yummy desserts, vinyl records and even listen to Jazz music here

BOGOBIRI HOUSE

In addition to being a 16 rooms boutique hotel that includes an art gallery, 2 restaurants and bars for the discerning traveller, Bogobiri offers a rich and unique array of entertainment and events. From the Thursday night “OPEN MIC NIGHT” and Tuesday night TARUWA which offers a platform for new talent in “spoken word” and music to the weekend High Life, Jazz and Afrobeat shows by more established performers. Bogobiri is a magnetic hub in which to eat lounge enjoy good music and connect with interesting people in a relaxed, chilled out environment.

RUFUS AND BEE

If you're looking to have fun, look no further than Rufus and Bee. Located in Twinwaters building, it has a games arcade including a bowling alley! You can't run out of things to do here and there's also a sports bar where sports lovers can sit back and watch their favorite games.

FELA SHRINE

Located in the capital of Lagos: Ikeja. The New Africa shrine renamed Fela shrine to honor legendary afrobeat icon Fela Anikulapo Kuti. This centre hosts a variety of events including performances by Femi Kuti and his band. You can indulge in a host of local delicacies like Jollof rice, Ayamase and ofada, peppered snails and lots more. Fela Shrine also hosts the yearly Felabration; a yearly music festival in honour of the late king of Afro music Fela Anikulapo Kuti. Any night is a good night. However, try and go when Fela's son plays.

ATV WORLD LAGOS

This place can also be referred to as quad biking heaven as the tracks here are specially made for quad biking. Who would say no to biking on the beach? Literally no one. They even offer trainings for newbies. You can have fun racing with friends or loved ones here.

LEKKI LEISURE LAKE

Lekki Leisure lake is a premier private beachfront in Lagos with a serene and beautiful environment. They offer a relaxing and enjoyable escape from the bustle of city life. Lekki leisure lake is also home to the prestigious Waves Sport Lounge, Ocean Terrace, Gabions and exotic event spaces for rent. You can plan and host any event at lekki Leisure beach, from beach weddings, to reception, birthday parties, and celebrations of all kinds.

FREEDOM PARK

Freedom Park is a memorial and leisure park area in the middle of downtown Lagos in Lagos Island, Nigeria which was formerly Her Majesty's Broad Street Prison. It was designed by the Architect Theo Lawson. The Park was constructed to preserve the history and cultural heritage of Nigerians and a peaceful place for contemplation and interaction.
